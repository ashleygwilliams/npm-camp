diversity!

npm loves you. we recognize that there are many people underrepresented in our community, and that circumstances often prevent such people from participating in events like npmCamp. to that end, we are offering free tickets to npmCamp.

if you are a member of an underrepresented group and would benefit from a scholarship to npmCamp, we would love for you to apply. some details about the process:

  • applications are confidential and will only be viewable by the applicant, the npmCamp organizers, and members of npm, Inc
  • we will do our best to accomodate all applicants, however in the event that we cannot we will do our best to provide the most benefit to the largest number of applicants
  • applicants will be notified of their status no later than April 1

Sponsor a Candidate

if you'd like to help us provide tickets and/or travel and accomodation grants to underrepresented people, please visit our sponsorships page and request to provide a Diversity Sponsorship.
