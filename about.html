about

npmCamp is a community conference about all things npm.

Are we going camping?

nope! npmCamp is called "camp" because it is our first conference. it's going to be smaller, and more intimate that a full blown conference. there won't be sponsor tables and we'll only have 200 attendees.

How can I get in touch with the organizers?

  • npmcamp@npmjs.com
  • @npmcamp on Twitter
